To save you the most time finding information across all of your tools, it's best to install our browser extension. It works for Brave, Chrome, Edge, Firefox, Safari, and Opera.

To do so, just visit [https://app.glean.com/setup](https://app.glean.com/setup) and you'll be stepped through the process for your browser.​You can also install directly from:

* [Chrome Web Store](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/glean/cfpdompphcacgpjfbonkdokgjhgabpij)
* [Firefox Browser Add-ons](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/glean/)
* [Safari](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/glean-for-safari/id6444195239)
