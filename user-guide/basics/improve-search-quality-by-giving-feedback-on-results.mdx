Help us improve search quality at your company by giving feedback on results.

Within your first few searches, you may see this little message in the top right corner asking for feedback about the quality of the results shown.

You can share feedback using that button at any time to help us improve search quality for your entire company. ✌️

You can also message us anytime if you have feedback on search quality - your feedback is always welcome and appreciated!
