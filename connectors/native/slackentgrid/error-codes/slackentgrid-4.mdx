## Issue:

- At least one of the workspaces that were specified are not valid.

## Resolution:

- Please verify that the workspace id you entered corresponds to a valid workspace.
- Please follow [https://slack.com/help/articles/221769328-Locate-your-Slack-URL-or-ID](https://slack.com/help/articles/221769328-Locate-your-Slack-URL-or-ID) to find you workspace id.

For additional assistance, please contact Glean Support at [https://support.glean.com](https://support.glean.com)
