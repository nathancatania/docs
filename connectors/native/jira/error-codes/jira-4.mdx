## Issue:

- The Glean Jira Activity plugin has not been installed on the Jira instance. The plugin is important for ranking and providing the best Glean experience

## Resolution:

- For Jira Data Center, please ensure that the latest version of the Glean plugin is installed on your Jira instance. You can install it from here

For additional assistance, please contact Glean Support at [https://support.glean.com](https://support.glean.com)
