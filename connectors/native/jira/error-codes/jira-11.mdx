## Issue:

- The Glean Crawler is unable to list groups for your Jira instance

## Resolution:

- For Jira Cloud, please ensure that the crawler app is installed and the group atlassian-addons-admin has 'Browse users and groups' global permission. More info here.
- For Jira Data Center, please ensure the service account has 'Browse users and groups' global permission. More info here.

For additional assistance, please contact Glean Support at [https://support.glean.com](https://support.glean.com)
