## Issue

We couldn't find a Google Drive connector instance connected to Glean that has the same domain as the Google Sites connector. We use this to fetch permissions for Google Sites documents.

## Resolution

Make sure that Google Drive for the same domain is connected to Glean

For additional assistance, please contact Glean Support at [https://support.glean.com](https://support.glean.com)
