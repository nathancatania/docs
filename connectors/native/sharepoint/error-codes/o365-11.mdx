description: O365-11 Error Code
---

## Issue:

- The pilot user group you specified is not valid. The pilot user group must have at most 100 people and not be a nested group.

## Resolution:

- Please navigate to the Azure portal.
- Verify that the group id you specified matches the one you entered.
- Verify that the group is not nested (contains other groups as members).
- Verify that the group contains at most 100 people.

For additional assistance, please contact Glean Support at [https://support.glean.com](https://support.glean.com)
