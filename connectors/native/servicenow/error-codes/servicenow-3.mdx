## Issue

- The Glean Search User time zone is not properly configured to GMT

## Resolution

- Under Organization > Users > gleansearchuser record, validate that the Time Zone configured to the user is GMT

For additional assistance, please contact Glean Support at [https://support.glean.com](https://support.glean.com)
