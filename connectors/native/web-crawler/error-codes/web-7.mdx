Seeds fetch validation

The seed URLs provided cannot be fetched successfully. Please verify that:

- The URLs entered are correct and can be opened in your browser.
- There are no firewall configurations that prevent the Glean crawler from accessing the website.
- The correct secrets are entered with the help of Glean support if the website requires authorization to access.

For additional assistance, please contact Glean Support at [https://support.glean.com](https://support.glean.com)
