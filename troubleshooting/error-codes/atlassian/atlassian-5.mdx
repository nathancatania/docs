## Issue

- The Atlassian Cloud domain entered for your instance is invalid.

## Resolution

- Please enter the Atlassian domain in the correct format. If your Atlassian instance url looks like `https://company.atlassian.net`, then your Atlassian domain is `company`.

If you are still having difficulty please reach out to us: [https://support.glean.com](https://support.glean.com)
