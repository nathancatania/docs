## Issue:

- The Glean Crawler Application isn't installed on your Atlassian Cloud instance

## Resolution:

- Please ensure you have entered your Atlassian domain correctly. If your Atlassian instance url looks like `https://company.atlassian.net`, then your Atlassian domain is `company`.
- Please install the Glean Crawler Application from [here](https://marketplace.atlassian.com/vendors/1216844/glean-technologies-inc).

If you are still having difficulty please reach out to us: [https://support.glean.com](https://support.glean.com)
