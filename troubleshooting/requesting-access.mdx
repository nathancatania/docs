<Note>
  If you need an account on the support portal, please contact your company's assigned Customer Success Manager.
</Note>

Glean's support portal access is restricted to:
- Glean Administrators who are Designated Support Contacts as defined in the [Glean Support Agreement](https://glean.com/support-agreement).

This focused access control ensures that users submitting issues to Glean Support are:
- Fully authorized to make requests on behalf of their company
- Able to collaborate effectively during support investigations 
- Equipped to assist in issue resolution
