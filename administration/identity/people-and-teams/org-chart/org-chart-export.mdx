## Overview

Glean provides administrators with the ability to export organizational chart data to CSV files, enabling easy access to structured employee information for analysis or integration with other systems.

## Export Process

### Accessing Export Options

As an administrator, you'll find the Export button next to the Org Chart settings. The export process follows these steps:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Starting Point">
    Navigate to the person whose organizational data you want to export
  </Step>

  <Step title="Initialize Export">
    Click the Export button to open the export configuration drawer
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure Export Options">
    Choose your export parameters:
    - Report scope: Include only direct reports or all reports
    - Employee filtering: Option to exclude contractors
  </Step>

  <Step title="Generate Export">
    Click the Export button in the drawer to download the CSV file to your local machine
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Exported Data Fields

The CSV export includes comprehensive employee information across various categories:

<Card title="Standard Fields">
- Email address
- Full name
- Preferred name
- Department
- Job function
- Location
- Employee type
- Job title
- Role type
</Card>

<Info>
Any custom fields configured in your Glean instance will also be included in the export.
</Info>

### Using the Export

The exported CSV file provides a structured view of your organizational hierarchy that can be used for:
- Organization analysis
- Team planning
- System integrations
- Reporting purposes

The data is downloaded directly to your default download directory in CSV format, making it easily accessible for further processing or analysis in spreadsheet applications or other tools.
