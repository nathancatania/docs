## Glean App Permissions by Role

If you are a Glean Super admin or Admin, you can enable anyone at your organization to create Glean Apps. Here is an overview of Glean roles and their Glean App permissions:

| Action | Member (end user) | Setup Admin / App Creator | Super admin / Admin |
|--------|------------------|--------------------------|-------------------|
| Create, edit, and delete an App | ❌ No | ✅ Yes | ✅ Yes |
| View configurations of Apps created by others | ❌ No | ✅ Yes | ✅ Yes |
| Edit and delete Apps created by others | ❌ No | ❌ No | ✅ Yes |
| Assign App Creator role to any Member | ❌ No | ❌ No | ✅ Yes |

## How to Assign the App Creator Role

Follow these steps to assign the App Creator role to any Member in your organization:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Teammates">
    In *Workspace*, go to *Teammates* and click *Edit* next to any teammate's name to whom you want to assign the App Creator role.
    
    <Frame>
      <img src="https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/955416507/57e7fcdbc2712e5d253100e7/Screenshot+2024-02-06+at+11.30.34+AM.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enable App Creator Role">
    In the modal that opens, toggle on the *App Creator* role. Then click *Save*.

</Steps>

<Info>
